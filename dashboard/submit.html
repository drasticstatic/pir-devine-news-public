📋 Guidelines

Full Submission Guidelines

Literature

Share your poetry, articles, or 12-step reflections. There is no word count limit, but please ensure your sharing aligns with the PIR® 12 Traditions. Pieces that reference specific individuals by name (other than public figures) should be reviewed for anonymity before submission.

Art & Photography

Please upload high-resolution images (JPG or PNG) that reflect your journey of recovery. Our team will resize and format your artwork to fit the newsletter template. Original files are archived in the committee's Google Drive.

Byline Preferences

  • Full name: Published exactly as entered on the form.
  • First name & last initial: e.g. "Written by Sarah M."
  • Anonymous: No name appears anywhere in the published newsletter.

Content Standards

All submissions are reviewed for alignment with the PIR® 12 Traditions and the spirit of the Fellowship. The Literature Committee reserves the right to suggest minor edits or, in rare cases, decline a submission that falls outside these guidelines — always with a respectful explanation.

What We Love to Publish

  • Personal stories of recovery and integration
  • Step-by-step reflections and working notes
  • Poetry inspired by plant medicine, healing, or the 12 Steps
  • Artwork and photography rooted in the recovery journey
  • Short essays on harm reduction, sponsorship, or service
