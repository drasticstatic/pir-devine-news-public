Submission Guidelines
✍️

Literature

Poetry, articles, and 12-step reflections. No word count limit. Please ensure your sharing aligns with the PIR® 12 Traditions.

🎨

Art & Photography

High-resolution images (JPG or PNG) that reflect your journey of recovery. Our team will resize for the template.

🔏

Anonymous or Credited

Choose full name, first name & last initial, or remain completely anonymous in the published newsletter.
