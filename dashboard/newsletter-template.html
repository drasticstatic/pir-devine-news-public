Psychedelics in Recovery™ · 12-Step Fellowship

About PIR®

Psychedelics in Recovery™ (PIR®) is a 12-step fellowship for people who embrace psychedelics and other forms of healing as an aid to their recovery. We welcome all who seek a mindful, ethical, and spiritually grounded path.

PIR® meetings are available worldwide — online and in person. All are welcome.

Find a Meeting

  • Online meetings: psychedelicsinrecovery.org/online-meetings
  • In person: psychedelicsinrecovery.org/in-person-meetings

Literature Committee

The Literature Committee (LitCom) develops, reviews, and disseminates written materials for the PIR® Fellowship — including this newsletter, member guides, FAQs, and the 12-step literature adapted for our community.

Volunteers are always welcome. No special expertise required — only a willingness to listen, collaborate, and serve with humility.
